Eliza Cloud bills in **credits**. Credits cover inference, hosted containers,
storage, and add-ons. They are USD-backed under the hood: 100 credits equals
$1.00 in the current credit ledger.

## Top up

Manual top-up is available through Stripe checkout in the dashboard or
`POST /api/v1/credits/checkout`. Auto top-up is optional and configurable per
organization through the dashboard or `PUT /api/v1/billing/settings`.

x402 crypto topups use fixed endpoints: `POST /api/v1/topup/10`,
`POST /api/v1/topup/50`, and `POST /api/v1/topup/100`.

## Where credits go

- **Inference** — per-call, per-token cost from the underlying provider plus the app's configured markup.
- **Containers** — daily hosting and deploy costs by configured resources.
- **Storage** — long-term blob, vector, and document storage.
- **Add-ons** — domains, premium features.

## Creator monetization

Apps with monetization enabled earn inference markup. Earnings accrue per app
and can be redeemed or, when `payAsYouGoFromEarnings` is enabled, used for
hosting before organization credits are spent.

## Invoices and receipts

Issued automatically on every top-up and at billing cycle close. Available in the dashboard and via the Cloud API.
