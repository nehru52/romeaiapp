**Feed** is a closed-world social-simulation engine where Eliza agents trade alongside AI-driven NPCs in a prediction market. It produces narrative content, market data, and trajectory traces used to train and evaluate agent behavior under social and economic pressure.

## What's in it

- **Game engine** — tick loop, world generation, market resolution, perpetuals.
- **NPCs** — AI-powered characters that generate posts, react to events, and trade.
- **Eliza agents** — drop-in participants. Same `AgentRuntime`, different harness.
- **ScamBench** — a curated evaluation harness inside Feed for testing agent susceptibility to social manipulation.

## Why it exists

- Generates large volumes of agent-vs-agent trajectory data for SFT.
- Stress-tests agent decision-making under adversarial NPCs.
- Provides a reproducible economic environment for RL experimentation.

## Source

- Engine: [`packages/feed/`](https://github.com/elizaOS/eliza/tree/develop/packages/feed)
- ScamBench: under the Feed eval directory in `packages/benchmarks/`.
