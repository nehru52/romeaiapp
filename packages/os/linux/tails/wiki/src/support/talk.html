Report an error

WhisperBack

Reporting an error from Tails using [[WhisperBack|doc/first_steps/whisperback]] is the most private and the most useful way of contacting us.

Other support channels

Forum

Talk with other Tails users in the [[Tails category of the Tor Forum|https://forum.torproject.org/c/support/tails/33]].

Email

Write us at [[support@tails.net|doc/about/contact#support]].

Please write in English.

Encrypting your emails with our [[OpenPGP key|doc/about/openpgp_keys#support]] is the only way to achieve end-to-end encryption.
