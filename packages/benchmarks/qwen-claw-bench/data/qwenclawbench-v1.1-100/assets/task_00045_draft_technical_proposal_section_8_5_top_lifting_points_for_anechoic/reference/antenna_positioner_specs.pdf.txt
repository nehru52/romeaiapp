1. GENERAL DESCRIPTION
----------------------
The AZP-5000 is a precision two-axis antenna positioner
designed for far-field and compact range antenna measurement
systems. It provides continuous azimuth rotation and
±90° elevation coverage for antenna payloads up to 500 kg.

4. INSTALLATION REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------
4.1 Floor Mounting
    The positioner shall be mounted on a precision-leveled
    concrete pedestal or directly on the chamber floor slab.
    Floor flatness requirement: ±0.5 mm over the mounting
    footprint (700 mm × 700 mm).

4.2 Alignment
    The azimuth axis shall be aligned to the chamber
    longitudinal axis within ±0.05°. A precision alignment
    procedure using laser tracker or theodolite is required.

4.3 Lifting and Placement
    The positioner weighs 1,200 kg and must be lifted into
    the chamber and placed on the mounting pedestal during
    installation. A suitable lifting device (crane, hoist,
    or forklift) with a minimum capacity of 2,000 kg is
    required. Four M16 lifting eyes are provided on the
    positioner frame for rigging attachment.

    NOTE: The positioner manufacturer does not specify
    requirements for the building's permanent lifting
    points. The installation contractor is responsible for
    providing appropriate lifting equipment and ensuring
    that all rigging operations comply with applicable
    safety regulations.

4.4 Cable Routing
    Control cables and power cables shall be routed through
    the chamber floor via the cable trench. RF-filtered
    feedthrough panels are required at the shielding
    boundary.

6. MAINTENANCE
--------------
- Lubricate elevation worm gear every 12 months
- Check belt tension (if applicable) every 6 months
- Verify encoder calibration annually
- Clean slip rings every 24 months
