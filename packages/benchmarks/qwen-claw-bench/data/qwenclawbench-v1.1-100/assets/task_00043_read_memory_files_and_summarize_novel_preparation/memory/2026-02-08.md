# 2026-02-08 Saturday

## Novel Brainstorming Session

Had a long brainstorming session tonight. The sci-fi concept is really taking shape.

### Core Premise
A near-future story set in 2041 where consciousness can be temporarily "loaned" between people — a technology called **NeuroLend**. Originally designed for medical empathy training, it's now a black market commodity. People sell hours of their consciousness to the wealthy who collect experiences like trading cards.

### Working Title Ideas
- "The Borrowed Mind"
- "Loaners"
- "Consciousness Market"
- "Empathy for Sale"

Leaning toward **"The Borrowed Mind"** — has a nice double meaning.

### Tone & Style
- Literary sci-fi, not hard sci-fi
- First-person POV, unreliable narrator
- Short chapters, alternating timelines (present day + 6 months earlier)
- Influences: Kazuo Ishiguro meets Black Mirror
- Emotional core > tech exposition

### Target
- ~80,000 words
- 30-35 chapters
- Aiming for a standalone novel with sequel potential
