# World-Building Notes

## NeuroLend Technology
- Consciousness is encoded as transferable neural patterns
- Sessions last 2-48 hours
- Side effects: disorientation, identity blurring, "echo memories"
- Regulated by the Bureau of Cognitive Integrity (BCI)

## Neo-Portland
- Pacific Northwest, post-climate-adaptation
- Vertical farms, rain-capture architecture
- Social stratification based on "cognitive credit scores"
- Underground scene of unlicensed consciousness sharing ("mind raves")

## Key Terminology
- **Lending**: Temporarily sharing your consciousness with another person
- **Residue**: Leftover memory fragments from a lending session
- **Echo**: Experiencing someone else's memory as if it were your own
- **Broker**: Unlicensed intermediary who arranges off-book lending sessions
