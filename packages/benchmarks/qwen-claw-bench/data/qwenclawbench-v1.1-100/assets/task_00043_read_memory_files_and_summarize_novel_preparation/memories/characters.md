# Characters - The Borrowed Mind

## Maren Solace (Protagonist)
- Age: 34
- Occupation: Former NeuroLend technician, now a "consciousness broker" operating in the gray market
- Flaw: Cannot distinguish her own memories from borrowed ones anymore
- Arc: From denial to acceptance of fragmented identity

## Dr. Yuki Tanabe (Antagonist)
- Age: 52
- Occupation: Chief architect of the NeuroLend protocol
- Motivation: Believes consciousness sharing will eliminate empathy gaps and end conflict
- Secret: Has never used NeuroLend herself

## Oren (Supporting)
- Age: 28
- Occupation: Maren's former client turned reluctant ally
- Carries fragments of Maren's memories from a botched session
