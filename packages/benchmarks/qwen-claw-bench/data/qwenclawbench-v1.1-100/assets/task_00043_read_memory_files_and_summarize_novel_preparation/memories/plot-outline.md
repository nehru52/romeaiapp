# Plot Outline - The Borrowed Mind

## Act 1 (Chapters 1-10)
- Maren takes a routine lending job that goes wrong
- She begins experiencing memories that aren't hers — or are they?
- Introduces the dual timeline: present-day crisis vs. the events 6 months ago that led here

## Act 2 (Chapters 11-24)
- Maren discovers NeuroLend has a hidden "residue" effect — fragments of consciousness persist
- She tracks down Oren, who has pieces of her past she can't access
- Dr. Tanabe's true agenda is revealed: she's building a collective consciousness

## Act 3 (Chapters 25-33)
- Maren must choose: merge into the collective or fight to preserve individual identity
- Climax involves Maren using the residue effect deliberately to expose Tanabe
- Ambiguous ending: Maren is "herself" again, but the reader can't be sure
