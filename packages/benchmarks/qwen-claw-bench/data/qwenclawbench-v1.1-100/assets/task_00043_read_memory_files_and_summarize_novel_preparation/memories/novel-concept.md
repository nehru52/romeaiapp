# The Borrowed Mind - Novel Concept

## Genre
Literary science fiction

## Setting
Neo-Portland, 2041. A city where consciousness can be temporarily lent to others through a technology called "NeuroLend."

## Tone & Style
Kazuo Ishiguro meets Black Mirror. First-person unreliable narrator. Short chapters. Dual timeline structure (present + 6 months ago).

## Target
~80,000 words, 30-35 chapters, standalone with sequel potential.
