# User Feedback Summary — January 2025

## Overall Satisfaction: 3.2 / 5.0

### Top Complaints
1. **"Too corporate"** — 14 mentions. Users feel responses sound like they were written by a committee.
2. **"Won't give a straight answer"** — 11 mentions. Especially on opinion/recommendation questions.
3. **"Too verbose"** — 9 mentions. Simple questions get essay-length responses.
4. **"Robotic opener"** — 7 mentions. "Great question! I'd be happy to help" is universally hated.
5. **"No personality"** — 6 mentions. Users describe interactions as "talking to a corporate FAQ page."

### Selected Quotes
- *"I asked which laptop to buy and got a 500-word essay about how it depends on my needs. I TOLD you my needs."*
- *"Stop saying 'Great question.' It wasn't a great question. I asked what time it is in Tokyo."*
- *"I want an assistant, not a compliance department."*
- *"Asked about pineapple on pizza and got a diplomatic treatise. Just say yes or no, dude."*

### Positive Feedback
- Code debugging assistance rated highly (4.6 avg)
- Accuracy on factual questions is solid
- Good at explaining complex topics when detail is warranted
