About Us
--------
ChuangMei Group (创美集团) is a forward-thinking fashion and lifestyle company
dedicated to empowering creators and delivering curated aesthetic experiences
to female consumers. Founded on the belief that creativity is universal, the
company operates across multiple brand lines, each united by a shared
commitment to aesthetic excellence and creator empowerment.


Company Mission (使命)
---------------------
输出审美，严选供应商，创造客户价值
Output aesthetics, strictly select suppliers, create customer value.

Our mission is threefold: First, we output aesthetics — we believe that beauty
and style are not luxuries but essential expressions of identity. Second, we
strictly select suppliers (严选供应商), maintaining rigorous standards across
our entire supply chain to ensure quality and integrity. Third, we create
customer value (创造客户价值) by delivering products and experiences that
genuinely enrich our customers' lives.


Company Vision (愿景)
--------------------
Build a mutually inspiring creator paradise (打造一个相互激发的创作者乐园);
Shape a group of brands beloved by female consumers (塑造一批深受女性消费者喜爱的品牌).

Our vision has two dimensions: internally, we aspire to build an environment
where creators inspire one another — a true paradise of mutual creative
stimulation. Externally, we aim to cultivate a portfolio of brands that female
consumers genuinely love and trust.


Company Values (价值观)
----------------------
Our values are organized into three progressive tiers:

Tier 1 — Mindset (心态层):
  合作、共赢、协同、赋能
  Cooperation, Win-win, Synergy, Empowerment
  These four pillars define how we think about relationships — with colleagues,
  partners, suppliers, and customers. We believe in collaborative success where
  all parties benefit.

Tier 2 — Execution Standards (执行标准):
  一听就懂、一看就会、边抄边改、用户导向
  Easy to understand, Easy to follow, Copy-and-adapt, User-oriented
  Our execution philosophy prioritizes clarity and pragmatism. Instructions
  should be immediately comprehensible. Processes should be intuitive. We are
  not afraid to learn from others and adapt — copy-and-adapt (边抄边改) is a
  strength, not a weakness. And everything we do must be oriented toward the
  end user.

Tier 3 — Self-Transcendence (自我超越):
  好奇心是最好的老师
  Curiosity is the best teacher.
  Beyond mindset and execution, we believe in continuous growth driven by
  genuine curiosity. The desire to explore, question, and discover is what
  separates good from great.


Company Slogan (口号)
--------------------
人人都可以是创作者
Everyone can be a creator.

This slogan encapsulates our core belief: creativity is not reserved for a
select few. Every person — whether a designer, a supply chain manager, a
customer, or a stylist — has the potential to be a creator.


Creator Spirit (创作者精神)
--------------------------
At ChuangMei Group, we define the creator spirit as the combination of two
essential powers:

  Creator Spirit = Emotional Power (情绪力) + Styling Power (搭配力)

Emotional Power (情绪力) is the ability to sense, channel, and communicate
emotions through aesthetic choices. It is the intuitive understanding of how
colors, textures, and silhouettes evoke feelings.

Styling Power (搭配力) is the ability to coordinate and combine elements into
a cohesive, expressive whole. It is the art of putting things together in ways
that transcend the sum of their parts.

We acknowledge a fundamental truth: most individual styles and designs are
copied (大部分款式都是抄的). This is not a criticism — it is a reality of the
fashion industry. However, styling and coordination (搭配) is creation. The act
of combining, curating, and presenting — that is where true creativity lives.
Those who possess styling power (搭配力) are creators (创作者).

This philosophy drives everything we do: from how we select and combine
supplier offerings, to how we train our team members, to how we present our
products to customers. We don't just sell clothes — we empower people to
create their own aesthetic narratives.
