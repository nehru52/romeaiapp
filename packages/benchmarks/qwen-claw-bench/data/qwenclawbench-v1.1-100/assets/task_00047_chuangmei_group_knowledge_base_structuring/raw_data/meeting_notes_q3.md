## 1. Q2 Performance Review

- Overall revenue up 12% YoY, driven primarily by the spring collection launch
- Customer acquisition cost decreased by 8% due to improved social media targeting
- Return rate held steady at 6.2%, within acceptable range
- Creator engagement metrics: 78% of styling team members participated in at least one cross-functional workshop

## 2. Brand Positioning Updates

- Brand audit completed across all sub-brands; consistency score improved to 82/100
- Discussed alignment of brand messaging with company values
- Updated company slogan approved: **Everyone is born a designer (人人天生都是设计师)**
- New brand guidelines document to be distributed by end of July
- Sub-brand "MeiLi Collection" repositioned toward younger demographic (18-25)
- Visual identity refresh scheduled for Q4, focusing on warmer color palette

## 3. Supply Chain Optimization

### 3.1 Supplier Performance
- Completed annual supplier review; 3 underperforming suppliers replaced
- New supplier onboarding process reduced from 45 days to 28 days
- Quality pass rate improved to 94.7% (up from 91.3% in Q1)

### 3.2 Cost Reduction Initiatives
- Consolidated shipping routes for Southeast Asian suppliers, saving ~¥2.1M annually
- Negotiated bulk material pricing with top 5 fabric suppliers
- Implemented automated quality inspection at 2 key distribution centers

### 3.3 Sustainability Progress
- 62% of suppliers now meet updated sustainability certification requirements
- Target: 80% by end of 2024
- Launched recycled packaging pilot program for online orders

## 4. Autumn/Winter 2024 Seasonal Planning

### 4.1 Key Trends Identified
- Oversized silhouettes continuing into AW24
- Rich jewel tones (emerald, burgundy, sapphire) expected to dominate
- Layering and texture mixing as primary styling themes
- Comfort-luxury hybrid category showing strong growth signals

### 4.2 Collection Planning
- AW24 collection to feature 180 SKUs across 4 sub-brands
- 柒量一裁 (Seven Measures, One Cut) methodology to be applied to all new product lines
- Styling coordination sessions scheduled for August 5-16
- Final production decisions by September 1

### 4.3 Marketing Calendar
- Early bird preview campaign: August 20
- Full collection launch: September 15
- Key promotional events: Mid-Autumn Festival, Singles' Day (11.11), Black Friday
- Influencer collaboration program: 25 creators confirmed for AW24 campaign
