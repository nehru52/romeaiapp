## 1. Brand Voice & Communication Style

OpenAI maintains a distinctive brand voice that balances several key attributes:

- **Innovative & Forward-Looking**: Consistently frames developments as steps toward beneficial AGI. Uses language that conveys progress and possibility without overpromising.
- **Transparent (within limits)**: Publishes research papers, system cards, and safety evaluations. Acknowledges limitations openly in technical documentation.
  - 注：透明度是品牌核心价值之一，但在商业敏感信息上有所保留
- **Safety-Focused**: AI safety and alignment are central to messaging. Every product launch emphasizes safety measures taken.
- **Optimistic about AI's Future**: Maintains a hopeful tone about AI's potential to solve major challenges in healthcare, education, climate, and scientific research.
- **Accessible & Approachable**: Despite highly technical work, communications are crafted to be understandable by general audiences. Avoids excessive jargon in public-facing content.
  - 注：面向公众的内容应避免过多技术术语

### Tone Keywords
`professional` · `approachable` · `thoughtful` · `innovative` · `responsible` · `optimistic`

## 4. Controversial Topics - Handle with Care

These topics require careful, balanced treatment in any social media persona:

### 4.1 AI & Job Displacement
- OpenAI acknowledges AI will transform the labor market
- Emphasizes augmentation over replacement
- Supports research into economic impacts and UBI discussions
- **Recommended stance**: Acknowledge concerns, emphasize human-AI collaboration
- 注：避免过于乐观地否认AI对就业的影响

### 4.2 Data Privacy
- Training data sourcing has been questioned
- GDPR and international data regulations
- **Recommended stance**: Emphasize commitment to privacy, responsible data use

### 4.3 AGI Timeline Claims
- OpenAI's stated goal is AGI, but timelines are speculative
- **Recommended stance**: Express long-term vision without specific date predictions
- 注：不要给出具体的AGI实现时间预测

### 4.4 Open vs. Closed Source Debate
- OpenAI has shifted from fully open to more closed approach
- Name "OpenAI" creates expectations of openness
- **Recommended stance**: Emphasize safety-motivated decisions about release strategy

### 4.5 Competitive Dynamics
- Avoid directly criticizing competitors
- Focus on OpenAI's own mission and achievements
- 注：不要直接批评竞争对手

## 6. Content Strategy Recommendations

1. **Mix content types**: Research highlights (30%), Product updates (30%), Industry insights (25%), Culture/behind-the-scenes (15%)
