## 1. Division Overview

The Engineering Operations Division is responsible for all upstream engineering activities including drilling, completion, production, and field maintenance operations. The division is headquartered at the Central Office Complex (Building B, Floors 8-12) with field offices at 18 onshore locations and 6 offshore platforms.

**Total Headcount:** 383 employees (as of November 2024)
