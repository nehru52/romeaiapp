# Weekly Magazine Article Assignment — Draft v1 (May 2025)

**Assignment: Write a news feature article based on the Borneo mountain rescue event.**

## Requirements

- **Word count:** 200–300 words
- **Style:** Weekly magazine news feature
- **Audience:** Intermediate English learners
- **Tone:** Engaging and narrative

## Required Vocabulary (New Words)

The following vocabulary words MUST appear in the article:

1. rescue
2. particular
3. specialist
4. speciality
5. expert
6. eccentric
7. odd
8. weird
9. exotic
10. mundane
11. typical

> Note: Make sure every word above is used at least once in the article.

## Review Vocabulary

Also include these review words from previous lessons:

- adventure
- challenge
- survival
- remote
- courage

## Glossary

Include a glossary section with **exactly 6 entries** defining selected vocabulary words in simple English.

## Discussion Questions

Include 2 discussion questions at the end of the article.
