PROJECT SCOPE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The project involves the basic design of a high-end polyolefin new materials
production facility. The plant will produce specialty polyethylene and
polypropylene grades for automotive, packaging, and medical applications.

Estimated Plant Capacity:  300,000 tonnes per annum
Estimated Duration:        18 months
